This site would not be possible without the hard work of the wiki editors at Last Haiku.

That being said, it would appear Last Haiku has been abandoned and I got tired of having to rely on Google to find the things I wanted on Last Haiku. I also found the color scheme hard to read on my aging eyes. While I could fix the colors locally, that wasn't appealing to me. Transfering the content from a wiki to a Drupal site was interesting to me, so here we are. I hope you find this site useful!

There are limits to what this site can do - you'll notice searching for "falcon" never returns a result. This is a limit of the platform and unless I win the megamillions, it's unlikely the added expense of upgrading the search server is going to happen. You can report other bugs here. No promises they'll be fixed, but I might!

All Fourth Edition PDFs can be found for sale on DriveThru RPG. I'd encourage you to purchase the source material as they are all excellent books and extremely high quality (even in PDF form).

Thanks for reading!
